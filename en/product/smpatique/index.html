Smpatique

A natural dietary supplement for slimming and weight loss assistance.

Smpatique from Salama Care is one of the latest slimming products used in several countries such as the United States, Canada, and South America, and is now available in the Syrian market.

Studies indicate that being overweight and obesity may lead to many health problems such as cardiovascular disease and diabetes, so products have been developed to help control weight safely and naturally.

Smpatique works by inhibiting the activity of the alpha-amylase enzyme responsible for digesting starches and converting them into absorbable simple sugars, which helps reduce calorie absorption and contribute to weight loss.

The product also helps slow the absorption of sugars and improve blood sugar control.

How the product works:

The alpha-amylase enzyme in the body converts complex carbohydrates into simple sugars such as glucose, which are absorbed in the intestines and then converted into energy or stored as fat.

Smpatique works on:

  • Reducing the activity of this enzyme
  • Slowing the absorption of sugars
  • Reducing fat storage
  • Supporting the weight loss process
