Properties and Mechanism of Action:

Glucosamine:

Glucosamine is a natural substance found in mucopolysaccharides, mucoproteins, and chitin. It is present in some foods such as shellfish and crab, but the dietary supplement is considered the best source to obtain an additional amount.

Glucosamine helps to:

  • Maintain cartilage flexibility
  • Support joint health
  • Promote hyaluronic acid production
  • Improve anti-inflammatory effect
  • Support reconstruction of damaged cartilage

Methylsulfonylmethane (MSM):

MSM is naturally found in fruits, vegetables, milk, meat, fish, coffee, and tea. It is an important source of sulfur involved in:

  • Formation of connective tissues
  • Supporting cartilage health
  • Improving joint flexibility

It may also help support osteoarthritis treatment, reduce cartilage wear, and improve joint function.

Composition:

Each two capsules contain:

  • Glucosamine Sulphate 500 mg
  • Methyl Sulfonyl Methane (MSM) 500 mg

Indications:

The product is used in the following cases:

  • Supporting joint health
  • Relieving joint pain
  • Increasing mobility
  • Supporting repair of damaged joints
  • Supporting osteoarthritis cases
  • Supporting joint disorders

Precautions and Warnings:

The product should be used with caution in the following cases:

  • Diabetes
  • High cholesterol
  • Gastric ulcers

Glucosamine may affect glucose regulation and insulin sensitivity.

Contraindications:

The product should not be used in the following cases:

  • Shellfish allergy
  • Pregnancy
  • Breastfeeding

Side Effects:

The product is generally considered safe, and some minor symptoms may appear such as:

  • Constipation or diarrhea
  • Heartburn
  • Nausea
  • Headache or drowsiness
  • Skin rash

Drug Interactions:

Theoretically, the effectiveness of insulin or some sugar-lowering drugs may decrease when using the product.

Dosage and Usage:

Recommended dosage:

  • Two capsules three times daily after food

It is recommended to use the product for more than 4 weeks to obtain the desired therapeutic effect.
